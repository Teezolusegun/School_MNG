SYSTEMATIC APPROACH

We follow the best approach to make our students the best they can be. We use a Project Based Learning(PBL) model an approach that focuses primarily on having students engage in explorations of real-world problems and challenge. Through these explorations, they develop their content knowledge, but also develop solutions to problems.

ONE TO ONE STUDY

Alpha school use the one-to-one education approach. We offer one on one study groups, one-to-one classroom study and also one student to one equipment including computers and laboratory equipments. This enable our students to have first hand contact with peers, their teachers and facility they need to become better students.
